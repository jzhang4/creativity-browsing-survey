This is a research study in which we are looking to see if we can predict your performance on creativity tasks based on your browsing patterns. You will first install a Chrome extension that will look at which sites you spend the most time on, and then you will answer some creativity related tasks. The tasks will take 5 minutes to complete total.

To begin, please install our extension here. The extension is open-source and will not share data without your consent. Your data will only be used for research by the Stanford HCI Research Group and will not be shared with other parties. You can uninstall the extension once you submit the HIT.

This research study is being conducted by Jessica Zhang jzhang4@stanford.edu . There are no anticipated risks or benefits to participants. The results of this research study may be presented at scientific or professional meetings or published in scientific journals.

If you have read this form and have decided to participate in this project, please understand your participation is voluntary and you have the right to withdraw your consent or discontinue participation at any time without penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled. The alternative is not to participate. You can refuse to answer particular questions, and your anonymity will be maintained.
